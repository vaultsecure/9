Introduction

Hey there! My name is Myth and I am a small aspiring graphics designer, roleplay-based builder, & project manager within the Roblox community - contributing to over 34,000,000 visits on the platform.

I have experience in owning, co-owning, and developing for a variety of different groups across the platform - more prominently within the roleplay community.

My aim when I started getting into development was to be able to provide a full package - building, scripting, GFX creation, management, discord management, & so on. I have gotten to grips with all but scripting, and I still intend on making my other skills such as graphic design and building better as I get more experience.

I wanted to deliver this package to groups that may need it the most, for, which may sound surprising, free of charge. I felt and somewhat do feel the work that I have done may not need such a heavy price tag since I see it as doing a favor for a group, which goes a lot further than Robux to me. Recently I have started charging due to me having responsibilities that need the income, such as groups I need to provide for.
